Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025


This Privacy Policy explains how Astrum Vera (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit or use our services.

1. Data We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, and contact messages. We also collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, referral sources, device info, and site usage via cookies and analytics tools.

2. Use of Data

• To operate and improve our services

• To respond to customer service requests

• To send newsletters or promotional materials (only with consent)

• To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance site performance, track usage patterns, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Processors

We engage third-party services (e.g., email, hosting, analytics) that process limited personal data under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your data. No system is entirely secure, but we maintain industry-standard measures.

6. Legal Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time. If you are subject to GDPR or CCPA, you may also request data portability and object to processing.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for operational or legal purposes.

8. Children

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted here with a revised date. Your continued use of the site constitutes agreement.

10. Contact

For any data concerns, contact us at contact@astrumvera.com.
