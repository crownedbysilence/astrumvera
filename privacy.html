Privacy Policy

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. If you are located in the EU or California, you may also have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA.

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.
